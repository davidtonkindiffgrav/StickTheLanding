    Australia Names Strong Squads for Home Oceania Championships and International Tour

    27 April 2026  ·  Stick The Landing

    TL;DR Australia has announced full men’s and women’s squads (senior & junior) for the Oceania Championships in Brisbane, plus selected athletes and coaches for upcoming events in Slovenia and China.

    Australia has named squads for the Oceania Artistic Championships (08-09 May, Brisbane), plus upcoming events in Slovenia and China. The Oceania event will feature senior and junior men’s and women’s teams competing at the Sleeman Sports Complex.
